# 🤝 NGO-Project

A comprehensive fund and donor management system designed to help NGOs streamline their operations, track donations, and maintain meaningful relationships with their supporters.

## 📋 Features

### 💰 Donation Management
- **Record Donations**: Log monetary donations and in-kind goods from supporters
- **Donation History**: Maintain a complete audit trail of all contributions
- **Multi-Category Support**: Track different types of donations (cash, products, services, etc.)

### 👥 Donor Management
- **Donor Database**: Store and manage detailed donor information
- **Donor Profiles**: Track donor history, preferences, and contribution patterns
- **Contact Management**: Organize donor contact details and communication history
- **Donor Analytics**: Gain insights into donor behavior and engagement

### 📧 Automated Communication
- **Thank-You Messages**: Automatically send personalized thank-you messages to donors
- **Email Notifications**: Keep donors informed about the impact of their donations
- **Customizable Templates**: Create organization-specific communication templates

### 📊 Financial Reporting
- **Comprehensive Reports**: Generate detailed financial reports and summaries
- **Donation Analytics**: Track donation trends and patterns over time
- **Fund Allocation Reports**: Monitor how funds are being utilized
- **Tax Documentation**: Generate reports suitable for tax and regulatory compliance

## 📖 Usage

### Recording a Donation
1. Navigate to the Donations section
2. Click "Add New Donation"
3. Enter donor information and donation details
4. Select donation category (cash, goods, services)
5. Submit to save and automatically generate thank-you message

### Managing Donors
1. Access the Donor Management dashboard
2. View, search, and filter donors by various criteria
3. Update donor information and communication preferences
4. Track complete donation history per donor

### Generating Reports
1. Go to the Reports section
2. Select report type (financial summary, donor analysis, fund allocation, etc.)
3. Choose date range and filters
4. Generate and download report in desired format
